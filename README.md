body
===================
> You've heard of 'head' and 'tail', well here's 'body'!

Print the body of a file, defaulting to the middle 10 lines.


```
 body <file>
```

currently prints out up to `10` lines of a files body

Building
===================
`make`

Installing
===================
`sudo make install`
